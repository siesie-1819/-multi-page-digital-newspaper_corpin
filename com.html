COMMUNITY ISSUES AND EVENTS AFFECTING SOCIETY

Top 5 Community Issues in the Philippines

  1. ☆ Flooding in Urban Communities: Many areas in the Philippines experience flooding due to heavy rainfall and clogged drainage systems.
  2. ☆ Waste Management Problems: Improper garbage disposal continues to affect environmental cleanliness and contributes to pollution.
  3. ☆ Traffic Congestion: Major cities face serious traffic problems because of increasing vehicles and limited road space.
  4. ☆ Disaster Preparedness: Communities must prepare for typhoons, earthquakes, and other natural disasters common in the country.
  5. ☆ Youth Participation in Community Development: Encouraging young people to join volunteer programs helps strengthen community leadership.
