Marikina’s Flood Control: A Model for Clean and Safe Communities

State Your Opinion Clearly

In my opinion, Marikina City has become one of the cleanest and most organized cities in Metro Manila. Because of the leadership of Mayor Marcelino “Marcy” Teodoro, flooding in the city is no longer as severe as before. The improvement of flood control systems and better monitoring of the Marikina River helped protect many communities during heavy rains.

Give Reasons

One reason flooding has improved is the construction of stronger riverbank linings and the regular cleaning of waterways. These projects help control the flow of water and prevent the river from overflowing during strong rainfall.

Suggest Solutions

To continue these improvements, the local government and residents should maintain proper waste disposal and support environmental programs. Keeping canals and drainage systems clean will help prevent flooding in the future.

Call to Action

Everyone in the community should participate in cleanup activities and help protect the environment. If citizens and leaders work together, Marikina can continue to be a model city for flood prevention and cleanliness.
